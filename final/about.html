Background
Raised in a Chinese-American household in the vibrant city of San Francisco, California, I've cultivated a nuanced cultural identity through the melding of diverse influences. This multicultural upbringing has honed my awareness of both cultural nuances and psychological subtleties. Within my experience, my passion lies in understanding human behavior at its cultural and behavioral core. As a designer, my mission is clear: to harness my skills to create products that are inclusive, accessible, simple, and immensely valuable to users from all walks of life.
Why Design?
As a creative explorer at heart, I've explored various disciplines during my academic journey, finding inspiration and fulfillment in the intersection of psychology, engineering, and technology. For me, design isn't just a profession; it's a canvas where I blend my passion for understanding human behavior, my knack for problem-solving, and my fascination with cutting-edge technology.
Through product design, I channel my inner child's boundless curiosity into tangible solutions that resonate with users on a deeply human level. Join me on this journey of creativity, innovation, and endless possibilities!
Who am I Beyond Design?
Beyond design, I find joy in the simple pleasures of life. Whether it's spending quality time with close friends or exploring uncharted areas, I embrace each moment with the enthusiasm of a tourist as I capture memories through photography, and savor new culinary experiences. While my passion for exploring cannot be understated, I also cherish the comfort of home, where I crochet and unwind with a good movie.
